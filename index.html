A recipe is a formula of ingredients and a list of instructions for creating prepared foods. It is used to control quality, quantity, and food costs in a foodservice operation. A recipe may be simple to complex based on the requirements of the operation and the intended user.

Work

The duty of foods and beverages product division (kitchen) is to make the raw materials become eatables. F&B Product division is also has support division to make the process of cooking becomes easier, that is stewarding division. Food and Beverage product is the first division which has responsibilities to control food and beverage activities in hotel, especially at banquet event, restaurant, and event order.

Food and beverage product has a lot of section such as: Butcher, Pantry, main kitchen, and stewarding. Butcher is a place where many kinds of meat are placed . Pantry is a place which used to prepare any condiment and garnish of food and beverage process. Main kitchen is a place where the cook activities are done, started from preparing the raw materials until processing of cooking, and stewarding is one of section which used to save main kitchen tools.

About

I am so glad that you are here.

First let me introduce myself. My name is Hema Rajesh a food blogger, food photographer, recipe creator and youtuber.

I am a happy homemaker, married to my high school sweetheart and blessed mother of two beautiful angels.

Yummy Tummy was started around 9 years back. I started my blog as a tiny little space where i can share my recipes with my friends and family. Later it turned into my career.

I used to cook different foods for my friends at work. They started asking for the recipe. That was a turning point for me, So i decided to start my very own blog where i can share my recipes. My work was a research based one and it went pretty good, but i found that something was lacking in that. I realized that this is not what i wanted to do in my life. After lot of thinking and discussion with my husband. I took a brave decision of quitting my job with my family's support. Yes I quit my job to continue my passion. This blog was a tiny one before, but as days passed it received lot of appreciation and my viewer count started increasing. I spent most of my time blogging. I cooked lot of food recipes and uploaded it in my blog daily. As years passed YUMMY TUMMY has become a part of my life.
